## AI SEO
Here are the key SEO changes in AI search for 2026:

- **Intent matters more than keywords**  
  Search engines focus less on exact phrasing and more on whether content fully solves the user’s goal.

- **AI Overviews reduce clicks**  
  More answers are shown directly in search results, so traditional organic traffic is declining.

- **Ranking is shifting to visibility and citations**  
  The goal is increasingly to be **referenced, cited, or included** in AI answers—not just ranked #1.

- **Topical depth and authority are critical**  
  Content needs to demonstrate complete coverage of a topic, not just surface-level optimization.

- **Entity-based SEO is growing**  
  AI systems understand brands, people, and topics as connected entities, so semantic relationships matter more.

- **Trust signals are stronger than ever**  
  Reviews, author expertise, brand mentions, consistency, and evidence of experience influence AI trust.

- **Structured data and machine readability matter more**  
  Clear site architecture, schema, summaries, and well-organized content help AI parse and use your pages.

- **Content that AI can’t easily replicate is more valuable**  
  Original research, primary data, lived experience, and unique POV are increasingly important.

- **Informational content is less about traffic, more about feeding AI**  
  Many top-of-funnel pages now serve as inputs for AI answers, while conversion-focused content becomes more important.

- **New metrics are emerging**  
  SEO success is increasingly measured by AI citations, branded search growth, share of AI conversation, and downstream conversions.
